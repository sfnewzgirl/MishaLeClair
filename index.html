WITHIN REACH

About the App

"Within Reach" is a mobile-friendly app geared toward people who rely on Dialectical Behavior Therapy as part of their recovery process. The goal is to make the hard work of recovery easy, accessible, and private. Users can create guided entries that walk them through the core tenants of DBT techniques including mindfulness, emotion regulation, and distress tolerance. The app allows users to share those entries with a chosen mental health professional to further aid in their recovery. Prior to "Within Reach" the main source of this work was a workbook, but this workbook is big, cumbersome, and awkward (who wants to whip out a big workbook with the word "bulimia" on the cover).

My Role

I pitched the idea for the "Within Reach" app and my cohort's selection of it moved me. It was one of only four app ideas selected for the group projects. I served as team leader during this one-week sprint in addition to building the models, views, controllers, and routes in Rails.

Technical Details

  • Ruby on Rails
  • jQuery
  • Materialize

PROTIPS

About the App

The ProTips App and API offers theme park visitors easy access to trip planning and crowdsourced advice they can't easily get elsewhere. While many resources explain rides, attractions and services (many even rate those), inevitably the best advice comes from another park visitor who has been there many times before and has a wealth of knowledge to share. Users can read ProTips, write their own, and vote up or vote down on a specific ProTip. Users can also comment on a ProTip to add their spin, additional information or even let other users know that ProTip is now outdated.

Live Link GitHub

My Role

I worked extensively on the server side and database during this pair progject - creating schemas, models, RESTful routes and server-side functions. For example, users vote up or down with separate button clicks on the front end that are sent through AJAX calls to the server, where a JavaScript function updates the database to persist the value.

Technical Details

  • HTML, jQuery
  • Node.js, Express, Mongoose, MongoDB
  • CSS, Sass

RACE TIME

About the App

A two player race against the clock. When a player clicks the "Ready to Play?" button, a countdown begins. Players are given 30 seconds to rack up the most clicks. Player 1 uses the "A" key and player 2 uses the "L" key.

Live Link GitHub

My Role

I built this game over one weekend as a exercise in jQuery and timing. I created a countdown for players with text displaying "ready, set, go". Also, I used keyup as the event listener so a player wouldn't be able to hold a key down and rack up points quickly as well as to avoid a tie if two players simply held their keys down.

Technical Details

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • jQuery
